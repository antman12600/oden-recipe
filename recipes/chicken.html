Chicken That's FINGER LICKEN GOOD

DESCRIPTION

Although there are multiple different ways to make chicken, we'll be focusing on GRILLED CHICKEN. We want to make sure we're staying on the healthier side (even though fried chicken is my favorite). However, just because it's healthy doesn't mean we can't make a BOMB RECIPE!

INGREDIENTS

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • Olive Oil
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon Juice

DIRECTIONS

  1. First, lay out your chicken thighs neatly on a plate
  2. Apply your seasoning and your lemon juice on the chicken so that it's evenly coated
  3. Once evenly coated, apply olive oil onto a pan and lay your thighs over medium-high heat
  4. Cook each side evenly until your chicken is nicely cooked(Make sure your chicken is thoroughly cooked to avoid salmonella!)
  5. Once completed, enjoy your HEALTHY but DELICIOUS chicken!
